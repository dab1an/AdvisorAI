## FIU Minors (CASE)
Source: https://case.fiu.edu/advising/case-minor/
Type: webpage

### Overview
FIU offers a variety of minors through the College of Arts, Sciences & Education (CASE), allowing students to complement their major with additional areas of study.

### Available Minors
Examples of available minors include:
- Astronomy
- Biology
- Chemistry
- Education (Professional Training Option)
- English
- Environmental Studies
- FIUteach (STEM Teaching)
- Geology
- Marine Sciences
- Mathematical Sciences / Mathematics
- Meteorology
- Philosophy
- Physics
- Psychology
- Statistics

### Key Information
- Students can request to add a minor through an online form
- Requests are reviewed to ensure:
  - Academic success
  - Timely graduation
- Some restrictions apply:
  - Psychology is the only minor available fully online

## Change Degree Program
Source: https://onestop.fiu.edu/registration/academic-records/change-degree-program/
Type: webpage

### Overview
Students can request changes to their academic program, such as switching majors, adding a second major, or modifying minors or certificates.

### Key Information
- Program changes depend on:
  - Student level (undergraduate or graduate)
  - Department approval
- Academic departments may accept or deny requests

### Undergraduate Program Changes
- Requests are initiated through an academic advisor
- Students must meet with an advisor in the desired program
- Possible changes include:
  - Changing majors
  - Adding/removing a second major
  - Adding/removing a second bachelor’s degree
  - Adding/removing a minor
  - Adding/removing a certificate

### Graduate Program Changes
- Requests must be submitted through the University Graduate School
- Applies to:
  - Degree program changes
  - Certificate or level changes
