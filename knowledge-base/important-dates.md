# Important Dates
Last Reviewed: 2026
## FIU Academic Calendar
Source: https://onestop.fiu.edu/academic-calendar/
Type: webpage

### Overview
The FIU Academic Calendar provides all important academic dates and deadlines for each semester, including registration, enrollment, holidays, and graduation-related deadlines.

### Key Information
- The calendar includes important dates for:
  - Fall, Spring, and Summer semesters
  - Registration and enrollment periods
  - Add/Drop deadlines
  - Withdrawal deadlines
  - Holidays and university closures
  - Graduation application deadlines
- Students can filter the calendar by semester

### Dynamic Nature of the Calendar
- The academic calendar is continuously updated
- Past events are removed over time
- Students should check the calendar regularly for the most current deadlines

### Available Calendar Types
- Current Academic Calendar (main student reference)
- Faculty Calendars (for instructional deadlines such as syllabus submission and course materials)
- Application Checklists (deadlines based on student type)
- Past Academic Calendars (archived versions)

### Graduation Deadlines
- The calendar includes deadlines to apply for graduation
- Missing these deadlines may delay graduation eligibility

### Key Usage Notes
- Students should review the calendar at the start of each semester
- It should be checked frequently throughout the semester to avoid missing deadlines
- The academic calendar is one of the primary sources for official university deadlines
